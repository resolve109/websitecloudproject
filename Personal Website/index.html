About Me

Cloud Solutions Architect-in-Training & Avid Explorer of Life's Many Facets

Hello world! I'm Siddharth Patel, but you can also call me a tech enthusiast, a history buff, and a lover of life's diverse experiences. While my professional journey has led me through the realms of cybersecurity and cloud technology, my personal path is equally rich and varied.

As an avid gamer, my journey began with a SEGA Genesis at the age of 10, and eventually evolved into building my own PCs. I'm currently on the quest for the perfect PC setup that I can proudly call my 'tech offspring' for the next decade.

When I'm not immersed in technology, you can find me indulging in my passion for golf, practicing swings at the range, or playing a full 18. It's not just a sport; it's my zen time.

My pup, a dog named Rani, is often by my side, sharing in life's adventures and quiet(very loud actually) moments alike (you can find videos of her in the gallery)

My fascination with world history and cultures often leads me down the YouTube rabbit hole, watching documentaries and expanding my understanding of the world. It's a testament to my belief that learning never stops.

Speaking of learning, I'm always working on self-improvement. Recent reads include "Crystal Clear Communication," "The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck," and "Unlimited Memory." These books reflect my commitment to growth, whether in communication, mindset, or cognitive skills.

And when I encounter something I don't know, I'm upfront about it. But that doesn't stop me. With resources like Google, ChatGPT, books, podcasts, and YouTube at my disposal, I'm always ready to learn, adapt, and provide answers. Ignorance isn't a barrier; it's a starting point for discovery.

Welcome to my world, where technology, gaming, history, and a quest for knowledge blend into a unique journey. It's a path of continuous learning, exploring, and appreciating the myriad aspects of life. Dive in and explore!
